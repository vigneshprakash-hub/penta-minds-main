Penta Minds — Brand Identity & Logo Usage Notice

This is the single authoritative brand identity and ownership reference for the entire Penta Minds ecosystem.

Authoritative Reference

Ownership Statement

Penta Minds — including its name, logo, visual identity, brand marks, research ecosystem identity, portal names, and all associated public-facing presentation — is the institutional identity of the Penta Minds student-led AI research organization, founded in 2026.

The Penta Minds logo and brand identity may not be used in any way that suggests authorization, endorsement, partnership, affiliation, representation, or official status without prior written permission from Penta Minds.

The Penta Minds name and visual identity are protected as the official institutional marks of the Penta Minds ecosystem, encompassing all portals operating under the pentaminds.in domain and subdomains.

Permitted Use

  • Referencing Penta Minds in academic, journalistic, educational, or collaborative contexts with proper attribution.
  • Linking to official Penta Minds portals and public research pages.
  • Using the Penta Minds name in factual descriptions, citations, and coverage.
  • Displaying the logo only when written permission has been granted, or when supplied through an official collaboration channel.
  • Quoting Penta Minds research outputs with source attribution under licensed terms (where applicable).

Prohibited Use

  • Modifying, redrawing, distorting, recoloring, or recreating the Penta Minds logo in any form.
  • Using the logo or name for commercial, political, misleading, or unrelated promotional activity.
  • Implying official partnership, certification, sponsorship, or endorsement without written approval.
  • Placing the brand identity on materials that conflict with Penta Minds' research, educational, or institutional purpose.
  • Reproducing the Penta Minds visual identity in contexts designed to misrepresent institutional affiliation.

Ecosystem Coverage

This notice governs the brand identity across all official Penta Minds portals, including but not limited to:

  • pentaminds.in — Core institutional site
  • research.pentaminds.in — Research Division
  • team.pentaminds.in — Team Division
  • projects.pentaminds.in — Projects Division
  • toolkit.pentaminds.in — Toolkit Division
